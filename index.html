Welcome to mahabaleshwar cream

Nestled in the heart of Mahabaleshwar, Mahabaleshwar Cream brings you farm-fresh indulgence from handcrafted ice creams and rich brownies to refreshing Smoothies, Mastani, Fresh juices, and our signature Strawberries, Blueberries and Mulberries with cream. Made with freshly plucked fruits, every bite celebrates real taste and freshness.
